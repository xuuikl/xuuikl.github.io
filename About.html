Design to create values.

Hi, I’m Kevin.

I love making themes for my phone, installing lots of tweaks to make the user flow more fluent, and often stay up late until I get the perfect look. I then found myself having great interest in user interface and user experience.

In college I majored in industrial design, since Industrial Design emphasizes good products is user-centered, I have spent more time on observing people’s need. Steve Jobs once said: “Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works.”

I am also interested in fashion, graphic design, interior design, and photography, which helps me to have better perspective in user experience.
